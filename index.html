The Network

In an increasingly globalized world, RCN harnesses blockchain technology to bridge credit’s supply and demand across borders, creating a new ecosystem for decentralized debt product.

The network connects credit markets’ stakeholders with key Decentralized Finance service providers, including ID verifiers, scoring agents and exchanges.
